**BI Requirements**


 **Business Intelligence Planning**

**Description**

This phase defines what information decision-makers need from the system. It answers the question:
“What should the system report and analyze?”

**Purpose**

Identify important business questions

Define required reports and summaries

Specify data sources from the database

Define user roles (admin, manager)

**Example Needs**

Total premiums collected per month

Number of active vs expired policies

Claim approval rates

Revenue by policy type
